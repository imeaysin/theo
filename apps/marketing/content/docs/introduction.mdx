Theo is the open source alternative to Loom. It is a lightweight, powerful screen recording tool that lets you capture your screen, camera, and microphone, then instantly share a link with anyone. No upload wait times, no file size limits, no friction.

Whether you are walking a teammate through a bug, recording a product demo, creating a tutorial, or sending async updates, Theo makes it fast and effortless.

## Why Theo?

**Open source and transparent.** Theo is built in the open with a permissive license. You can inspect every line of code, contribute features, or self-host on your own infrastructure.

**Instant sharing.** Record and get a shareable link in seconds. No waiting for uploads to finish or files to process.

**Professional editing built in.** Studio Mode gives you a full editing suite with background customization, zoom effects, trimming, and more, right inside the app.

**AI-powered captions.** Theo automatically generates accurate captions for every recording, making your content accessible and searchable.

**Cross-platform.** Theo Desktop runs natively on macOS and Windows. The web app works in any modern browser for viewing, managing, and sharing your recordings.

**Privacy-first.** Self-host Theo on your own servers for complete control over your data, or use our managed cloud service at [Theo.so](https://theo.example).

## Key Features

- **Instant Mode** - Click record, capture your screen, and get a shareable link the moment you stop. Perfect for quick walkthroughs and async communication.

- **Studio Mode** - Record locally, then edit with professional tools. Customize backgrounds, add zoom effects, trim footage, and export polished videos.

- **AI Captions** - Automatic transcription and captioning powered by AI. Every recording gets searchable, accessible captions.

- **Camera Overlay** - Include your webcam as a floating overlay to add a personal touch to any recording.

- **Team Sharing** - Share recordings with your team through workspaces. Viewers can leave timestamped comments and emoji reactions.

- **Analytics** - Track who is watching your recordings, how long they watch, and when they engage.

- **Embeds** - Embed your recordings in websites, documentation, and apps with a simple iframe snippet.

- **REST API & Webhooks** - Integrate Theo into your workflow programmatically with a full API and real-time webhook notifications.

## Getting Started

The fastest way to get started with Theo:

1. **[Install Theo Desktop](/docs/installation)** on macOS or Windows
2. **[Record your first Theo](/docs/quickstart)** in under a minute
3. **Share the link** with anyone

## Desktop App

Theo Desktop is available for **macOS** (Apple Silicon and Intel) and **Windows** (10 and later). Download the latest version from [Theo.so/download](https://theo.example/download).

The desktop app handles recording, camera/microphone capture, and local editing through Studio Mode. Recordings can be shared instantly through the Theo cloud or your self-hosted instance.

## Web App

The Theo web app at [Theo.so](https://theo.example) is your dashboard for managing recordings, customizing share settings, viewing analytics, and collaborating with your team. It is also where viewers watch shared recordings.

## Self-Hosting

Theo can be fully self-hosted on your own infrastructure using Docker Compose. This gives you complete ownership of your data, recordings, and user accounts. See the [self-hosting guide](/docs/self-hosting) for detailed setup instructions.
